Scrambled eggs Recipe

The favorite food is scrambled egg, I like this food because it is healthy

Ingredients needed

  • 2 eggs
  • cup of milk
  • cheese
  • oil

Steps to prepare

  1. Spray the pan with oil 😀
  2. Add two eggs to the pan 🥚🥚
  3. Add milk 🥛
  4. Let it cook for one minute😄
  5. Serve it on a plate😁
  6. Sprinkle cheese on top
